Features

A platform complete for your business

All the tools you need to manage your short-term rentals, in one elegant and powerful interface.

Channel Manager

Sync Airbnb, Booking, Abritel, Vrbo and other channels in real time. Prices, calendars, availability: everything is centralized.

AI messaging

The AI replies to guests 24/7 in multiple languages, in your brand's tone. You take over whenever you want.

Cleaning schedule

Automatically assign missions to teams based on check-outs. Live tracking, notifications, absence management.

Owner portal

A dedicated space for your owners: full transparency on bookings, revenue and automatic monthly statements.

Digital welcome book

Create personalized welcome books your guests view in one click: rules, codes, amenities, local tips.

Deposit & services 0%

Collect deposits and sell add-on services (early check-in, extra cleaning…) with zero commission. Payments via Stripe.

Inventory management

Track linens, consumables and equipment. Automatic alerts when stock drops below a threshold.

Multilingual

Your guests automatically receive messages in their language. FR, EN, ES, IT, DE and more.

Statistics & reporting

Visualize your occupancy rate, revenue, performance per property. Export data in one click.

Electronic invoicing 2026

GuestLucky generates invoices compliant with France's 2026 e-invoicing reform. Certified PDF, automatic sending, 10-year archiving.

Hoguet law compliance

Built for concierges holding the Card G: owner contracts, transaction register, separate accounting.

Auto-actions

Automatically trigger actions: send key box code 2h before arrival, request review 24h after departure, payment reminders, etc.

Provider mobile app

Your cleaning teams receive missions on their phone, complete checklists, take photos and send their invoice in one click.

Excluded words & AI filters

Define keywords the AI must never use or topics it must escalate directly to a human (dispute, refund, urgent).

Direct booking website

Receive commission-free bookings directly from your own website (optional). Native sync with your Channel Manager.

Flexible setup

Customize every detail: message templates per channel, dynamic pricing rules, cancellation policies, cleaning fees, tourist taxes.

Services & partners

Even more integrated services

GuestLucky continuously adds trusted partner services to cover 100% of your concierge needs.

Booking website

Booking In Shine

Create your personalized direct booking website with zero commission. Your guests book directly with you, in your branding, logo and terms. Automatic sync with GuestLucky's Channel Manager.

Revenue Management

PriceLabs (official partner)

Automatically optimize your prices with PriceLabs, our revenue management partner. Real-time market analysis, demand-based price adjustment, local events handling: maximize your revenue effortlessly.

Home automation

Smart lock management

Control your smart locks directly from GuestLucky: automatic generation of unique codes per booking, secure send to the guest, deactivation at check-out. Compatible with major brands on the market.

Insurance

Lucky Cover

Our Lucky Cover insurance protects your properties against guest damages, last-minute cancellations and revenue loss. Peace of mind included for hosts and professional concierges.

Security & compliance

Your data, secure

GuestLucky meets the highest standards of the European SaaS industry.
